## 2. Risk assessment methods

- **Qualitative:** likelihood/impact rated (Low/Med/High), **risk matrix/heat map** â€” fast, subjective.
- **Quantitative (know the formulas):**
  - **SLE** (Single Loss Expectancy) = **Asset Value Ă— EF** (Exposure Factor).
  - **ARO** = Annualised Rate of Occurrence (times/year).
  - **ALE** (Annualised Loss Expectancy) = **SLE Ă— ARO**.
  - Justify a control if its annual cost < the **reduction in ALE** it delivers.
- **Risk register:** living record of risks, owners, scores, treatments, status.
- **Assessment types:** one-time / continuous / ad-hoc; **control assessment**, **risk-based**.

## 8. Audits, assessments & third-party risk

- **Audit vs assessment:** an **audit** checks conformance against a defined standard/control set
  (pass/fail, often by an independent/external party); an **assessment** evaluates posture and risk
  more broadly (findings + recommendations). **Internal vs external/independent** changes assurance
  weight.
- **Attestation & evidence:** **SOC 2 Type I** (controls *designed* at a point in time) vs **Type II**
  (controls *operating effectively* over a period); right-to-audit clauses in contracts.
- **Security testing types:** vulnerability assessment, **penetration test** (black/grey/white box â€”
  domain 1 Â§5), **red/blue/purple team**, **bug bounty**, tabletop exercises.
- **Third-party / supply-chain risk:** vendor due diligence/questionnaires, SLAs and the agreements in
  Â§6, continuous vendor monitoring, **fourth-party** (your vendor's vendors), EOL/EOSL dependency
  risk, software provenance (SBOM â€” domain 3 Â§8).

## 9. Security metrics, KPIs & reporting

- **Operational metrics:** **MTTD** (mean time to detect), **MTTR** (mean time to respond/recover),
  dwell time, alert volume / false-positive rate, patch latency, % assets compliant with baseline.
- **Risk indicators:** **KRI** (key risk indicator â€” leading signal a risk is rising) vs **KPI**
  (performance against a target); track residual risk and control coverage.
- **Reporting up:** translate technical findings into business risk for the risk register and board â€”
  the communication skill consultancies and SOCs screen for (interview point).
